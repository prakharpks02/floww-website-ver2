Frequently Asked Questions

The place to answer all the questions and queries related to Floww.

  • For Transporters
  • For Brands
  • For Drivers
  • Is the Floww App user friendly for operation/warehouse managers?

    The Floww App is designed to be simple and user-friendly, just like WhatsApp while featuring all the logistics solutions in one place. You can upload multiple orders at once via excel sheet and take charge of entire order management.

  • Is Floww transport management app free to use?

    Floww App is free to download. The basic version of app is free to use, add-on features can be purchased for a nominal cost.

  • Can I configure payment collection options on the Floww app?

    Yes, you can configure any collection options you might require on the the Floww app. Options for money collection via bank account or UPI can be enabled and connected to Floww's generated website (Storefront)

  • Can I send the tracking link to my customers to help them keep tabs on the last-mile rider?

    Yes, tracking of the order can be done in real time by end customer, depending on different triggers such as fixed ETA or completion of previous delivery.

  • Can I track my fleet members with tracking link?

    Yes, you can track every member on your fleet for better resource management Status tracking is free for fleet | Real-time tracking is chargeable.

  • Not getting notification of new orders?

    Sometimes notifications would get paused if the app is already open, or if nothing works, try logout and then login again.

  • Can I send ETA-based SMS to a customer?

    Yes, Floww can help you send ETA-based SMS notifications to a customer via Notification Intelligence

  • How Floww can help with transport related documentation?

    Floww can help transporters to generate all transport related documents such as e-way bill, LR, POD based invoice, GST, delivery challan, claim settlement documents at nominal charges.

  • I have a slotted delivery system, can the Floww take care of the same?

    Yes, Floww can help you optimize the routing plans for slotted deliveries

  • Can I send order tracking link to customers for real-time updates?

    Yes, With Floww, you can send tracking links to your customers and always keep them updated with their orders

  • I don’t have my own fleet, can I integrate my 3PL with Floww?

    You can integrate with Floww verified or choice of your own /existing transporters with standardized Floww APIs.

  • Can Floww help if I am using 'daily market hire' vehicles?

    Floww can suggest the vehicle options with the best costing to be used in case of daily hires. Can be connected via Clip-on GPS devices or a smartphones.

  • I have vehicles already installed with GPS devices, can Floww take inputs from these devices?

    Floww can integrate with any existing GPS vendor and take inputs such as lat/longs, timestamps, speed, temperature, rpm, etc.

  • What to do if Image is not uploading in Rider App?

    You can click WhatsApp button in the order detail page to open chat with your manager and send image on WhatsApp.

  • Receiver is not picking up the phone?

    Inform the manager on WhatsApp, the manager can confirm delivery from their Manager App in case OTP can not be accessed.

  • Not getting notification of new orders assigned?

    Sometimes notifications would get paused if the app is already open, or if nothing works, try logout and then login again.
